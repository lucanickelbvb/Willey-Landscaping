About Us

Willey Landscaping LLC

Willey Landscaping is a local lawn care company proudly serving Rochester Hills, Michigan. Founded by Alec Willey at just 15 years old, the company was built on a simple belief: your yard deserves the same level of care and attention as every other part of your home. With a strong focus on quality, reliability, and detail, Willey Landscaping is committed to helping homeowners maintain clean, healthy, and well-kept outdoor spaces they can take pride in.

Whether it's a weekly mow or a full fall cleanup, we treat every property like it's our own. No shortcuts, no excuses — just honest work and a yard you'll be proud of.
